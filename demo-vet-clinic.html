Our Team

Vets who truly care

Paws & Care was founded in 2013 by Dr. Sara Al-Rashid, a veterinarian with a passion for building a clinic that puts patients — and their families — first. We've grown into a team of seven specialists, each with international training and a genuine love for animals.

Our philosophy is simple: treat every pet like it's our own. From the first checkup to complex surgery, we maintain the highest clinical standards with the warmth of a family practice.
